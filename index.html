What is Mythic+?

Mythic+ is a system in World of Warcraft that allows players to challenge dungeons with increasing difficulty levels. Players use a "keystone" to unlock Mythic+ dungeons, and the goal is to complete them within a set time limit. As the keystone level increases, the difficulty and rewards also grow.
